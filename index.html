    Transportation

    Moovit

    Moovit is a public transportation app that provides real-time transit information, route planning, and navigation for buses, trains, and other transit modes. It aims to simplify urban mobility by offering live updates, schedules, and alerts for efficient commuting.

    Waze

    Waze is a GPS navigation app that provides real-time traffic updates and turn-by-turn directions using crowd-sourced data from its users. It helps drivers avoid traffic, find the fastest routes, and receive alerts about accidents, hazards, and police traps.

    Gett

    Gett is a ride-hailing app that connects users with professional drivers for on-demand transportation and delivery services. It offers reliable rides and efficient logistics solutions, focusing on business clients and corporate accounts.

    Google Maps

    Google Maps is a web mapping service that offers detailed maps, real-time traffic conditions, and route planning for traveling by foot, car, bicycle, or public transportation. It also provides information on nearby businesses and points of interest.

    Communication

    WhatsApp

    WhatsApp is a messaging app that allows users to send text messages, make voice and video calls, and share media and documents. It offers end-to-end encryption for secure communication.

    Google Translate

    Google Translate is a language translation service that translates text, speech, images, and websites between numerous languages. It provides instant translations and features such as pronunciation guides and real-time voice translation.

    Duolingo

    Duolingo is a language-learning app that offers interactive lessons and exercises in various languages. It uses gamification to make learning engaging and effective.

    Telegram

    Telegram is a messaging app that provides fast and secure communication through text, voice, and video messages. It features cloud-based storage, large group chats, and strong encryption for privacy.

    Safety

    Tzofar

    Tzofar is an alert system app that provides real-time notifications about rocket attacks and other emergencies in Israel. It helps users stay informed and safe by delivering timely alerts based on their location.

    Red Alert

    Red Alert is an app that provides real-time notifications of rocket and missile attacks in Israel. It helps users stay safe by delivering instant alerts based on their location, allowing them to take immediate protective action.

    Job Searching

    LinkedIn

    LinkedIn is a professional networking platform that connects professionals, allowing them to build and maintain professional relationships, showcase their skills and experience, and seek job opportunities. It serves as a platform for networking, recruitment, and professional development.

    Indeed

    Indeed is an online job search platform that connects job seekers with employers by aggregating job listings from various sources, such as company websites, job boards, and staffing firms. It offers tools for job seekers to search for positions, upload resumes, and receive alerts about relevant job openings.

    Glassdoor

    Glassdoor is a platform that provides insights into companies, including employee reviews, salary information, and interview experiences. It offers job listings, company profiles, and tools for job seekers to research companies and find employment opportunities.
